About Me


I am attending a Full Stack 12-week Coding Bootcamp at UT Austin from which I will graduate in October 2017. I'm currently building a network and am searching for a position in full stack web development. While attending this Bootcamp, I am concurrently finishing my MA in Human Dimensions of Organizations at the University of Texas. Prior to that, I earned my BS in Zoology, a BA in Liberal Arts with a minor in Computer Science. I have spent almost 30 years in patient care with a focus on pediatrics. During this time, I learned that while patient management is widely researched and explained, leadership in healthcare begs for more exploration.

This brought me to my studies at the University of Texas where I hope to translate my new understanding about organizations and the ethical, psychological and sociological way we treat our employees into a more productive and engaged workforce. While allied health is where I have honed my skiles in human relations, I am making a shift into technology to allow further growth and learning potential. I love solving problems and helping others relate to change with a positive reflection on the future.

In my spare time, when I'm not solving elusive web development puzzles, I enjoy time with my supportive husband and three dogs. Fun for me is to train and compete in canine agility.
